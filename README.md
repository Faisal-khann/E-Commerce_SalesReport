# Business Questions & Analysis Goals

This project aims to explore and analyze sales data to answer the following key business questions:

1. **Compare the sales and orders using a single chart**  
   - Goal: Create a combined visualization (dual-axis chart or line vs bar) to show the relationship between total sales and number of orders.  
   - Expected Insight: Understand whether higher sales are driven by more orders or higher-value purchases.  

2. **Which month got the highest sales and orders?**  
   - Goal: Perform monthly trend analysis of sales and orders.  
   - Expected Insight: Identify peak seasonality and best-performing months for business planning.  

3. **Who purchased more in 2022 ‚Äì men or women?**  
   - Goal: Segment customers by gender and compare purchase counts and revenue.  
   - Expected Insight: Understand gender-based buying behavior to improve targeted marketing.  

4. **What are the different order statuses in 2022?**  
   - Goal: Analyze distribution of order statuses (e.g., Delivered, Cancelled, Returned, Refunded).  
   - Expected Insight: Identify operational performance and areas for improvement.  

5. **List the top 10 states contributing to the sales**  
   - Goal: Rank states by revenue and orders.  
   - Expected Insight: Discover high-performing regions and focus on potential expansion areas.  

6. **Relation between age and gender based on number of orders**  
   - Goal: Build a demographic profile combining age groups and gender distribution.  
   - Expected Insight: Recognize the most active buyer segments and plan demographic-based marketing.  

7. **Which channel is contributing to maximum sales?**  
   - Goal: Compare performance across sales channels (e.g., Online, Offline, Retail, Marketplace).  
   - Expected Insight: Determine the most effective channel for revenue generation.  

8. **What is the highest selling category?**  
   - Goal: Identify product categories contributing maximum sales and orders.  
   - Expected Insight: Optimize inventory and promotion strategies based on top categories.

## Analysis and Insights

### Orders vs Sales
- Line and column chart showing monthly total sales and order counts.
- **Peak sales:** March
- **Trend:** Slight decline mid-year, stabilization toward year-end.

### Sales: Men vs Women
- Pie chart showing gender distribution:
  - Women: **64%**
  - Men: **36%**
- **Insight:** Women are the primary customer segment.

### Order Status
- Pie chart showing order outcomes:
  - Successfully delivered: **92%**
  - Refunded/Returned/Canceled: **8%**
- **Insight:** High delivery success indicates operational efficiency.

### Orders: Age vs Gender
- Bar chart categorizing buyers by age group and gender:
  - Adults dominate purchases (especially women, **34.59%**)
  - Teenagers: **21.13%**
  - Seniors: Smallest segment
- **Insight:** Adults are the major age group driving sales.

### Orders: Channels
- Pie chart representing sales distribution across platforms:
  - Amazon: **35%**
  - Flipkart: **22%**
  - Myntra: **23%**
  - Meesho, Ajio, Nalli: **4‚Äì6%**
- **Insight:** Amazon is the most effective sales channel.

### Sales: Top 5 States
- Column chart showing top-performing states by sales revenue:
  1. Maharashtra: 2.99M
  2. Karnataka: 2.65M
  3. Uttar Pradesh: 2.10M
  4. Telangana: 1.71M
  5. Tamil Nadu: 1.68M
- **Insight:** Regional marketing should focus on Maharashtra and Karnataka.
