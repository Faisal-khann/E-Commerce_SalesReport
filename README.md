## Analysis and Insights

### Orders vs Sales
- Line and column chart showing monthly total sales and order counts.
- **Peak sales:** February
- **Trend:** Slight decline mid-year, stabilization toward year-end.

### Sales: Men vs Women
- Pie chart showing gender distribution:
  - Women: **64%**
  - Men: **36%**
- **Insight:** Women are the primary customer segment.

### Order Status
- Pie chart showing order outcomes:
  - Successfully delivered: **92%**
  - Refunded/Returned/Canceled: **8%**
- **Insight:** High delivery success indicates operational efficiency.

### Orders: Age vs Gender
- Bar chart categorizing buyers by age group and gender:
  - Adults dominate purchases (especially women, **34.59%**)
  - Teenagers: **21.13%**
  - Seniors: Smallest segment
- **Insight:** Adults are the major age group driving sales.

### Orders: Channels
- Pie chart representing sales distribution across platforms:
  - Amazon: **35%**
  - Flipkart: **22%**
  - Myntra: **23%**
  - Meesho, Ajio, Nalli: **4â€“6%**
- **Insight:** Amazon is the most effective sales channel.

### Sales: Top 5 States
- Column chart showing top-performing states by sales revenue:
  1. Maharashtra: 2.99M
  2. Karnataka: 2.65M
  3. Uttar Pradesh: 2.10M
  4. Telangana: 1.71M
  5. Tamil Nadu: 1.68M
- **Insight:** Regional marketing should focus on Maharashtra and Karnataka.
